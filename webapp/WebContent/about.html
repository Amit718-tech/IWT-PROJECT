About Our Election Portal

The College Election Portal is designed to digitize the election process at Silicon University. It allows students to vote securely from anywhere while ensuring transparency and fairness. This system reduces manual effort and enhances the efficiency of election management.

Secure Voting

Ensures each student votes only once with proper authentication.

Real-Time Results

Votes are counted instantly and results are displayed live.

Easy Access

Students can vote from anywhere.
